8-4 Strats

2 Frame 1 Frame

2 Frame

Let go of B before coming out of the pipe and walk off of it. Before landing, repress B. After the pit, jump when the back of Mario is about lined up with the start of the floor. Do a bit less than a full jump. This should give 2 frames to jump off the pipe and gets 159 or 160 entry.

1 Frame

Easier

Run off the pipe and full jump on frame 170. In the air, start holding D. After landing, jump on frame 231. Do a medium speed wiggle when jumping up to the pipe. Optimal is frame 138 entry.

Intermediate

Start holding D in the air. After landing, jump on frame 233. Do a faster wiggle when jumping up to the pipe. Optimal is gets 137 entry.

Hard

Run off the pipe, do not hold down. Before landing after the pit, release right. When you land, press left for 1 frame and immeditly switch to right and jump. The wiggle can lose at most 1 subpixel. This can get 136 entry.
